Trig Clock

Why?

I just think it's kinda neat. It's based on simple trigonometry:

  • 00:00:00 is 0°, 06:00:00 is 90°, and so on.
  • Therefore, roughly, first quarter is late night, second is morning, third is afternoon, fourth is evening and early night.
  • Also, it doesn't require you to guess if it's AM or PM.

so I happen to find it an elegant alternative to the "traditional" analog clock.

Also I was bored, and it's not like things need a reason to exist.

I also find the counterintuitive idea of a anti-clockwise clock amusing :^)

How?

I made a desktop version of this using Pygame, and this version uses PyJS and Pyjsdl to compile it to Javascript.
